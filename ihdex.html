About Acto leboratory

ATCO Laboratories Limited is one of the pioneers in Pakistan’s pharmaceutical industry sector, and has been serving the country’s healthcare system over the last four decades. Currently, ATCO is 8th Largest Manufacturer on Unit Basis as per IMS 2ND QTR. 2022 of Pakistan and growing its business at much faster growth rate than the industry. ATCO started exporting its products in 1992 and now present in more than 23 countries.
